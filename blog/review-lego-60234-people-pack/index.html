Review LEGO 60234 ~ People Pack Fun Fair

April 14, 2020

Minifigures are one of the things my daughter loves the most about LEGO. She uses minifigures to create real world, and fantastic scenes; looking at her playing with minifigures is one of the best thing.

When we visit our local LEGO store, we cannot exit before having a stop at the “Build a Mini” tower - and I’ve to admit - that’s also because of me üòÖ

Having been locked down for almost 2 months now, we didn’t have the opportunity to visit our local LEGO store recently; so when I stumbled upon LEGO People Pack Fun Fair ~ LEGO 60234 on Amazon, and it had a 28% discount, I couldn’t resist from buying it.

So, let’s find out if we did a good deal. TL;DR YES, we did.

LEGO 60234 Fun Fair contains 14 minifigures, and the bricks to build some typical fun fair games. Each minifig has their accessory, and we got some unique piece in our collection:

The buildings were simple enough that my daughter (6yo) could complete them almost autonomously. I said almost autonomously cause is quite hard for me to just watch her playing LEGO, without building something myself.

In the end, this is how all the completed minifigures look like:

We took also some close shot.

Probably a very common accessory, but that was our first hotdog in our collection. We also liked a lot the style of the girl, and her hair in particular.

My daughter appreciated the little girl with the tiger on her face, cause when there’s a party she always like to get something painted (often unicorns) on her face.

The girl that sells ice creams wears a hat, but she still has blonde hair. Also the little girl’s hair looks like the one of her parents… these are just little details - much appreciated.

Finally the box contains some bricks to build some typical fair games. The ice cream cart is the one we liked the most.
