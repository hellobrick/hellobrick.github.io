A social-distant weekend

March 16, 2020

Sunday, March 15. Spring is sprung early this year; we can feel the sun is warm outside our windows, and hear chirping of birds. Perfect weather to stay at home, and play LEGO.

Oh, no… I am not kidding.

Ok, I admit, we like LEGO, and everything, but in similar condition, usually playing with LEGO would be the latest thing we would do. Anyway, this weekend is different, cause there’s coronavirus emergence in our country, and our government has recommended to avoid going out if not necessary.

… and we decided to make our small part, by staying at home, and playing LEGO of course.

What have we built? The cave of wonders!
